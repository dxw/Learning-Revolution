If you are running events that could be part of the Learning Revolution Festival in October, there are lots of ways to promote them

Add your events to this website

The main way to promote your events is by adding your events to the website. This involves filling in a simple form with the event details.

The sooner you get your events listed the better, as it will give learners more chance of finding them.

Download resources to promote your events

There are a whole host of resources to help you promote your events — such as logos, example web copy and a template press release. More resources will be added soon, so keep checking the page for the latest updates.
