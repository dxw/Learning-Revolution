Approval of submitted events

Material submitted to the website for publication will be reviewed by moderators prior to publication. Moderators will be monitoring the site between 9am- 5pm Monday - Friday from 28 September to 2nd November and will endeavour to process material within 72 hours of submission but can offer no guarantees that it will be processed or published within that time. We may edit submitted material before including it on the site.

Moderation is intended to be fair and inclusive, and will not be used to suppress legitimate informal learning opportunities.

We will normally approve material for publication as long as it:

  • Is not malicious or offensive in nature, and does not constitute a personal attack on a person's character;
  • Doesn't incite hatred on the basis of race, religion, gender, nationality or sexuality or other personal characteristic;
  • Doesn't include learning activity that is of a sexual nature or condones the sex industry or encourages gambling and smoking;
  • Doesn't include swearing, hate-speech or obscenity;
  • Doesn't break the law. This includes libel, condoning illegal activity, and breaching copyright;
  • Is reasonably concise, and doesn't constitute spam;
  • Doesn't solely advertise commercial products and services without a free or low cost option. You can mention relevant products and services;
  • Doesn't impersonate or falsely claim to represent a person or organisation;
  • Is in English. Unfortunately, we do not currently have the resource to moderate material in other languages;
  • Is relevant. Please don't post material that is unrelated to this site;
  • Is not party political in nature.

We reserve the right to suspend use of this website and services relating to it. We reserve the right to amend the policy above at any time. The decision of the moderators is final.
