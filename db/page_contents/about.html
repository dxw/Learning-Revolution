Learning for pleasure is about discovering new things, building your skills and knowledge, and getting the most out of your free time.

The Learning Revolution Festival celebrates all sorts of hobbies, clubs and evening classes and the difference they can make to people's lives. It includes everything from sports to art galleries, car maintenance to rambling, dancing to acting and more — so you're bound to find something that interests you.

Throughout October, hundreds of open events will highlight the variety of learning that's taking place and give you a chance to try something new. The Learning Revolution can help you discover enjoyable activities and learning in your area, whatever your budget or location.

If you would like to know more, check out our frequently asked questions.

Get involved!

Taking part is easy. You can find events near you on this website.

If you are a group organiser, leader or teacher you can add your event to the website or use our resources to help promote your event.
